Gentle Guidance for First-Time Parents

Life with Your Newborn

Life has changed now that your baby is here, and you might have lots of questions about what to do. These tips can help first-time parents feel confident about caring for a newborn in no time.

Getting Help

Consider getting help from relatives or friends. Their experiences can be valuable as you adjust to your new life.

Handling Your Baby

Wash your hands, support your baby's head, and avoid rough play. These basics keep your baby safe and happy.

Bonding

Cradle and gently stroke your baby or try skin-to-skin contact for emotional connection and development.

Soothing

Massage, calming sounds, and swaddling can help relax your baby and ensure they feel comfortable.

Diapering

Babies will dirty about 10 diapers a day. Learn how to keep your baby clean and comfortable.

Bathing

Start with sponge baths until the umbilical cord stump and circumcision area (if applicable) heal.

Feeding

Feed your baby on demand. Whether breastfeeding or bottle-feeding, keep a steady schedule.
