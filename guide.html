Postpartum Guide & Tips for Mothers

1. Rest and Recovery

  • Prioritize rest to allow your body to heal. Sleep when your baby sleeps.
  • Avoid overexertion—delegate chores or hire help if possible.
  • Practice proper posture while breastfeeding to avoid back pain.

2. Nutrition and Hydration

  • Consume a balanced diet rich in protein, iron, calcium, fiber, and healthy fats.
  • Drink plenty of water and herbal teas to stay hydrated and boost milk production.
  • Include fresh fruits and vegetables to support recovery and overall health.

3. Baby Care Tips

  • Breastfeed every 2–3 hours or on demand using proper latch techniques.
  • Bond with your baby through skin-to-skin contact.
  • Follow safe sleep practices: place the baby on their back in a crib.

4. Postpartum Hygiene

  • Keep stitches or cesarean wounds clean by following medical advice.
  • Take warm baths to relieve soreness and relax muscles (if allowed).
  • Use maternity pads and change them frequently to maintain hygiene.
