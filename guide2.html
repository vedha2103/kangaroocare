8 Things Every New Mom Should Know

Gentle Tips for New Moms to Navigate Parenthood

Life with Your Newborn

As a new mom, you’re likely feeling a mix of excitement, joy, and maybe even a little anxiety. This is a big transition, and it's okay to feel overwhelmed. These tips will help guide you as you embrace this wonderful new chapter of your life.

Getting Help

Don’t hesitate to ask for support from loved ones. Whether it’s family or friends, they can offer valuable guidance as you adjust to life with your newborn.

Handling Your Baby

Support your baby’s head and be gentle with their delicate body. Holding your baby carefully promotes a sense of security and safety.

Bonding

Bonding with your baby is essential. Cradle them in your arms, talk to them, and enjoy those precious moments together.

Soothing

Try massaging your baby gently, playing calming sounds, or swaddling them to help them relax and feel comforted.

Diapering

Newborns go through many diapers a day. Learn to handle diaper changes with care, keeping your baby clean and comfortable.

Bathing

Start with sponge baths until your baby’s umbilical cord stump falls off. Make bathing a relaxing, enjoyable routine.

Feeding

Whether you’re breastfeeding or bottle-feeding, feeding your baby on demand helps establish a healthy routine.

Sleep

Newborns sleep a lot, but in short bursts. Make sure to follow safe sleep practices to keep your baby safe while resting.
